About me

Your Trusted Language and Business Facilitator in China

I'm Monica Meng, a professional translator and interpreter based in Guangzhou, China. Fluent in English, Chinese, and Spanish, I specialize in assisting foreign clients in trade fairs, factory inspections, and business meetings. With a background in linguistics and extensive experience in diverse industries, I provide high-quality translation services tailored to your needs.

My services

Business and Factory Guide

I accompany foreign clients to factories and corporate offices across industries such as electronics, jewelry, textiles, and construction materials. I assist in negotiations, product inspections, and supplier meetings while providing expert translation and cultural insights.

Event and Conference Interpreter

As a skilled interpreter, I offer both consecutive and simultaneous translation for trade negotiations, business conferences, and high-level corporate events. I specialize in sectors like renewable energy, medical technology, real estate, and industrial equipment.

Exhibition and Trade Show Translator

With experience in major trade fairs such as the Canton Fair and the Hong Kong Jewelry Show, I help international businesses connect with potential partners, facilitating communication in client meetings and contract discussions.

Media and Fashion Industry Translator

I provide translation services for fashion shoots, advertisement productions, and model contracts. My expertise ensures clear communication between international models, photographers, and production teams, including negotiations for portrait rights and licensing agreements.
